PAREDS

PAREDS is a program to broaden participation in STEM by offering paid full-time summer research experiences to all physics and astrophysics majors who are also Charles Drew Science Scholars at Michigan State University. In addition to a great summer job, PAREDS participants benefit by establishing mentoring relationships, building a cohort of like-minded students, and partaking in professional development workshops and other enrichment opportunities.
